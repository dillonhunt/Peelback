Description of Service

Peelback is an ingredient analysis app that:

  • Scans product labels using your device's camera
  • Reads ingredient lists using on-device text recognition
  • Analyzes ingredients using AI to provide safety assessments
  • Personalizes results based on your allergens, dietary preferences, and medications
  • Allows manual product searches by name

AI-Generated Content

How AI Is Used

Peelback uses artificial intelligence (Anthropic's Claude) to analyze ingredient lists and provide safety assessments. All ratings, explanations, and recommendations you see in the app are generated by AI.

Limitations of AI

  • AI can make mistakes and may misinterpret ingredients
  • AI knowledge has a training cutoff date and may not reflect recent research
  • AI cannot detect manufacturing changes, cross-contamination, or unlisted ingredients
  • Results may vary between scans of the same product

Your Responsibility

AI-generated content is provided as a starting point for your own research. You should verify important information independently and consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

Important Disclaimers

Not Medical Advice

Peelback is for informational purposes only. The app does not provide medical, nutritional, or health advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions, especially regarding allergies, food sensitivities, or medication interactions.

Accuracy Limitations

  • Ingredient analysis is generated by AI and may contain errors
  • Product formulations change; our information may not reflect the latest version
  • Text recognition may misread labels, especially if damaged, blurry, or in unusual fonts
  • "Brand recall" results (when scanning packaging without visible ingredients) are based on AI training data and may not reflect current formulations

Not a Substitute for Label Reading

Always read actual product labels, especially if you have severe allergies. Do not rely solely on Peelback for allergy or safety decisions.

No Guarantee of Safety

A "Safe" rating does not guarantee a product is safe for you. It indicates the AI found no obvious concerns based on the information provided. Cross-contamination, manufacturing changes, and other factors are not detectable by scanning ingredients.
