Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a product?

Point your camera at the ingredient list on any product and tap the scan button. The app will read the ingredients and analyze them for you.

What do the ratings mean?

Safe means no concerns were found. Caution means some ingredients may need attention. Avoid means ingredients match your allergens or have significant concerns.

How do I set up my health profile?

Go to the Settings tab and tap on your profile. You can add allergens, dietary preferences, and medications to personalize your scan results.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your profile and scan history are stored only on your device. Images are processed for analysis and immediately deleted from our servers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Peelback and tap Cancel Subscription.

Why can't the app read my ingredient list?

Make sure the text is in focus, well-lit, and flat. Curved surfaces, damaged labels, or unusual fonts may affect accuracy.
